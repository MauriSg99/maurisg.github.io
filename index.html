MY STORY

A little piece of who I am and what I've done.

I am an aspiring Web Designer and Developer. Throughout highschool I always struggled with what I wanted to do with my life; what I wanted to study and where. I had many options and none of them seemed to please me.

I always loved design, but I knew that the life of a Graphic Designer is always very complicated. I took courses on 3D Design and borrowed my sister's camera to learn photography and filmmaking. What can I say, I like art. I started working as a freelancer but mostly designing Powerpoint Presentations for other people. Amazingly I earned more than I would imagine, but I knew that wasn't enough. And so it began my journey to learn coding.

Before FreeCodeCamp I only made one website, it sucked. But now, after the first few courses I've come to realize that I love coding. The feeling of creating something that wasn't there before. So hopefully, in a few more years my coding levels improve. So stick around because someday I am going to be great.
